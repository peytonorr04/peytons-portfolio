About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Cal State LA with experience in full-stack development, distributed systems, and data-driven engineering. I currently serve as the Project Manager and Lead Developer for a senior design team partnered with Makapo Aquatics, where I oversee IoT sensor integration, backend processing, and frontend visualization for a real-time paddling analytics platform. My work spans Unity game development, scalable backend systems, and human-centered UI design. I enjoy building projects from concept to deployment and using software engineering principles to solve meaningful,real-world problems.

Projects

Makapo Aquatics

Makapo Aquatics is an adaptive paddling organization supporting athletes with visual impairments. Our team created a full pipeline that collects paddle stroke data from IoT sensors, processes it through a central data hub, and delivers meaningful insights to coaches through a user-friendly app interface.

Debugged

Debugged is a fast-paced, narrative-driven 3D couch co-op game built in Unity, where players take on the roles of digital characters fighting to save a corrupted computer from glitch outbreaks. Designed for 2 players, the game blends action combat, team-based mechanics, skill checks, and environment puzzles across multiple handcrafted levels.

Django Bookstore

Worked on a full-stack Django web application as part of a group project for a computer science course. The app is designed like an online bookstore, similar to Barnes & Noble. It lets users browse books, add items to a shopping cart, favorite books, leave comments, and give star ratings.
