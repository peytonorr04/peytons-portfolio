Makapo Aquatics — Senior Design Project

About The Project

Makapo Aquatics is an adaptive paddling organization supporting athletes with visual impairments. Our team created a full pipeline that collects paddle stroke data from IoT sensors, processes it through a central data hub, and delivers meaningful insights to coaches through a user-friendly app interface. The system captures live training data from prototype sensor units mounted on the paddles. This data is transmitted through a secure broker to our Makapo Data Hub, which parses, cleans, and formats the information before sending it to the mobile-friendly coaching dashboard. Coaches can then analyze athlete performance, track consistency, and give real-time feedback during practice sessions.
